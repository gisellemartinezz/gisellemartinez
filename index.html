Giselle

I write and record music.

My name is Giselle. I appreciate quality music and strive to create just that. As a solo artist, I sing originals accompanied by guitar and ukulele.

My love of music started at a very young age and I liked to express myself through it. It was a goal of mine to make it my own. Once I began to play musical instruments, it was easier to accomplish. Recently i have been writing originals and post them on sound cloud. I am located in the bay area of california and am available for performances.
